About

Hi, I'm Josh Parker and I'm a full stack web developer primarily focused on the MERN stack. I first discovered coding a couple of years ago and absolutely fell in love with it. Since then, I have pursued this passion in every way possible, trying to move my career in the direction of full time developer. After taking advantage of free online resources to teach myself, I decided it was time for a more formal education if I was going to get to the where I wanted to go. This past July, I graduated from Georgia Tech's Full Stack program and am now looking to put my skills to use and take the next step in my development career.
